About MyLittleJarvis

Transforming e-waste into intelligent assistants. Our mission is to democratize AI by making it accessible through devices you already own.

Our Mission

Every year, millions of smartphones end up in landfills. At the same time, powerful AI models like Claude, GPT, and Llama are becoming increasingly capable but remain inaccessible to many due to cost barriers.

MyLittleJarvis bridges this gap. We believe your old phone deserves a second life as your personal AI assistant—handling your scheduling, coding alongside you, managing your digital life, all while you save money and reduce e-waste.
